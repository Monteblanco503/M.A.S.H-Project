Learn how to play

Welcome to the game M.A.S.H! Follow these instructions to get started and enjoy your experience!

Follow these instructions

  1. M.A.S.H is the name of the game, and it's also one of the categories that'll predict your future: Mansion, Apartment, Shack, House.
  2. Decide your topics: These categories are crucial for crafting your unique 'future' scenarios but make sure to have fun with it as there are many topics to choose from. You can have as many categories as you like.
    Here are some common categories to choose from:
    • Career
    • Salary
    • Number of kids
    • Car
    • Pet
  3. Within each category, list up to 4 options or scenarios. These can range from being silly, horrible, too honest, or whatever you like!
  4. Next, with the press of a button , a random number will be generated at the top of the screen.
  5. This number will go through each category, cycling through the options entered to select your Future.
  6. Finally, read your future out loud. If you don't like it, just play again!

    Will you live in a shack with a million-dollar salary?
    Will you be picking up your 25 kids from school in your Lamborghini?
    How do you think your husband, Drake, will feel after you adopt a giraffe?
